### **Step 3: Set Up Payout for the DSA**

- In the **DSA Profile Page**, check if a **Payout** is already configured.
- If not, click on **Payout** and enter the necessary details.
- Submit the payout setup to proceed.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image4.png)

### **Step 4: Enable Direct Claim Access**

- Locate the **Direct Claim Access** checkbox.
- Check (âœ”ï¸) the box to grant **Instant Claim** access to the DSA.
- A **Digital Payout Configuration Popup** will appear.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image5.png)

- Configure the **Digital Payout Details** and click **Submit**.

### **Step 5: Confirmation**

- The **DSA now has access** to process **Instant Claims** on eligible products.
