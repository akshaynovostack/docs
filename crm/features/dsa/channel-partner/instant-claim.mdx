# **Allowing Instant Claim for a DSA**

## **Objective**

To grant **Instant Claim** access to a **DSA (Direct Sales Agent)** and ensure payouts are set up for seamless claim processing.

## **Steps to Enable Instant Claim for a DSA**

### **Step 1: Access the Channel Partner Section**

* Log in to the **CRM Dashboard**.  
* Click on the **hamburger menu** (☰) and go to **DSA \> Channel Partner**.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image1.png"/>

### **Step 2: Search for the Preferred DSA**

* Use the **search bar** to enter the DSA’s name.  
* Click on the **Eye Icon (👁️)** next to the DSA’s name to open their profile.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image2.png"/>

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image3.png"/>

### **Step 3: Set Up Payout for the DSA**

* In the **DSA Profile Page**, check if a **Payout** is already configured.  
* If not, click on **Payout** and enter the necessary details.  
* Submit the payout setup to proceed.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image4.png"/>

### **Step 4: Enable Direct Claim Access**

* Locate the **Direct Claim Access** checkbox.  
* Check (✔️) the box to grant **Instant Claim** access to the DSA.  
* A **Digital Payout Configuration Popup** will appear.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/dsa/channel-partner/instant-claim/image5.png"/>

* Configure the **Digital Payout Details** and click **Submit**.

### **Step 5: Confirmation**

* The **DSA now has access** to process **Instant Claims** on eligible products.
