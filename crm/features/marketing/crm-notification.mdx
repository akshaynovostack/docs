#

## **Steps to Send CRM Push Notifications**

### **Step 1: Navigate to CRM Notification Service**

1. Go to the **Marketing** tab in the application.

2. Click on **CRM Notification Service** to open the feature.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/crm-notification/image1.png)

### **Step 2: Select Notification Template**

1. Choose an **existing template** from the available list.

2. Alternatively, create a **new template** based on your preferences.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/crm-notification/image2.png)

### **Step 3: Apply List Filter**

1. Select a **List Filter** to refine the target audience.

2. The system will display the **total count of customers** who will receive the push notifications.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/crm-notification/image3.png)

### **Step 4: Send Push Notification**

1. Click on **Send Push Notifications** to initiate the message dispatch.

2. The notifications will be successfully sent to the selected customers.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/crm-notification/image4.png)
