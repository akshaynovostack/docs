#

## **Steps to Send App Notifications**

### **Step 1: Navigate to App Notification Service**

1. Go to the **Marketing** tab in the application.

2. Click on **App Notification Service** to open the feature.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/app-notification/image1.png)

### **Step 2: Select Notification Template**

1. Choose an **existing template** from the available list.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/app-notification/image2.png)

1. Alternatively, create a **new template** based on your preferences.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/app-notification/image3.png)

### **Step 3: Select Notification Receiver Type**

1. Choose the **Type of Receiver** for sending notifications:

   * **Channel Partners**

   * **Customers**

### **Step 4: Apply List Filter**

1. Select a **List Filter** to refine the target audience.

2. The system will display the **total count of customers** who will receive the app notifications.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/crm/features/marketing/app-notification/image4.png)

### **Step 5: Send App Notification**

1. Click on **Send Notification** to initiate the message dispatch.

2. The notifications will be successfully sent to the selected customers via the **RU Connect App**.

## **Conclusion**

The **App Notification Service** simplifies the process of sending notifications to targeted customers or channel partners. By leveraging pre-defined templates and advanced filtering options, marketing users can efficiently manage and distribute notifications at scale.
