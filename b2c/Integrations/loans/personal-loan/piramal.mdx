# Steps to Apply for Piramal Personal Loan

1. **Launch the B2C App.**
2. **Click on the Personal Loan icon.**
3. **Basic Details Form:**  
   You will be redirected to the Basic Details Form. All details are mandatory except the “Middle Name.”
4. **Continue:**  
   Click the **Continue** CTA.
5. **OTP Verification:**  
   Enter the 4-digit OTP sent to your registered number, or the OTP will be auto-filled.
6. **Select Address:**  
   Choose from your saved addresses, or add and edit your address as needed.
7. **Select Income Details:**  
   Choose from your saved income details, or add and edit your income information.
8. **Loan Details Screen:**  
   Enter the desired loan amount and select the loan tenure from the drop-down menu.
9. **Loan Providers Screen:**  
   After submitting the loan details, you will be redirected to the Loan Providers Screen.  
   Select **Piramal Personal Loan** as your lender and click on either:  
   - **Continue with Applied Amount**  
   - **Continue with Offered Amount**
10. **Additional Details Screen:**  
    Complete the following details:
    - **Category:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Religion:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Qualification:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Address Category:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Income Mode:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Sales Person:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Employer Type:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Current Employment Duration:** (in months)
    - **Monthly Net Income.**
    - **End Use:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Income Address Type:** Select from the drop-down.
    - **Income Address Category:** Select from the drop-down.
