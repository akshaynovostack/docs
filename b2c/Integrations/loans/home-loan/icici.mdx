# Steps to Apply for ICICI Home Loan

1. **Launch the B2C App.**
2. **Click on the Home Loan icon.**
3. **Basic Details Form:**  
   You will be redirected to the Basic Details Form. All details are mandatory except the “Middle Name.”
4. **Continue:**  
   Click the **Continue** CTA.
5. **OTP Verification:**  
   Enter the 4-digit OTP sent to your registered number, or the OTP will be auto-filled.
6. **Select Address:**  
   Choose from your saved addresses, or add and edit your address as needed.
7. **Select Income Details:**  
   Choose from your saved income details, or add and edit your income information.
8. **Loan Details Screen:**  
   Enter the desired loan amount and select the loan tenure from the drop-down menu.
9. **Loan Providers Screen:**  
   After submitting the loan details, you will be redirected to the Loan Providers Screen.  
   Select **ICICI Home Loan** as your lender and click on either:  
   - **Continue with Applied Amount**  
   - **Continue with Offered Amount**
10. **References:**  
    Select “2 References” or add a new reference (or edit an existing one) as needed, then click the **Continue** CTA.
11. **Additional Details Screen:**  
    Complete the following details:
    - **Communication Consent:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **Religion:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **Property Occupation Status:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **Property Status:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **Total Tax Paid Annually.**
    - **Net Income.**
    - **Total Income.**
    - **Spouse Name.**
    - **RM Selection.**
    - **Company Name.**
    - **Builder:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **Category:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **PAN NSDL Status:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **City Description:** Select from the drop-down menu.
    - **Age of Retirement.**
    - **Property Address Line 1.**
    - **Property Address Line 2.**
    - **Property Value.**
    - **Email.**
