## Introduction

This document provides a comprehensive step*by*step guide for DSAs to apply for a loan using the RuConnect platform. It highlights mandatory and optional fields, supported by screenshots, to ensure clarity and completeness.

## Step 1: Access the RuConnect Platform

**URL:** Open the RuConnect platform at [https://ruconnect.in/](https://ruconnect.in/).

**Login**

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image1.png)

## Step 2: Navigate to the Dashboard

After successful login, you will land on the Dashboard.

**The Dashboard provides:**

* **Quick Access Links:** Links to personal loans, home loans, and other products.
* **Status Overview:** Displays details of ongoing or past applications.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image2.png)

## Step 3: Entering a Pincode

The user is prompted to enter their pincode, which helps determine location*based vendor options.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image3.png)

## Step 4: Select the Loan Provider

On the "Please Select The Provider" page, a list of available loan providers is displayed.

Examples include **Piramal, DMI, ICICI, Motilal, etc.**

Select **DMI Housing Finance** to proceed.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image4.png)

## Step 5: Customer PAN & Mobile Number Entry

This screen collects the customer's **mobile number and PAN (Permanent Account Number)** for identity verification and loan processing.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image5.png)

## Step 6: Provide Basic Details

### **Mandatory Fields:**
* Full Name
* PAN Number
* Gender
* Email
* Date of Birth
* Marital Status
* Father’s Name
* Mother’s Name

### **Consent:**
* Select checkboxes for Privacy Policy and Credit Information consent.

Click **Continue** to proceed.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image6.png)

## Step 7: OTP Verification

Enter the **One*Time Password (OTP)** sent to the registered mobile number for verification.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image7.png)

## Step 8: Enter Address Details

### **Provide your residential address:**
* House/Flat Number (Mandatory)
* Street/Locality (Mandatory)
* City, State, and PIN Code (Mandatory)

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image8.png)

## Step 9: Fill Your Income Details

### **Fields to Fill:**
* **Income Type:** Choose salaried, self*employed, etc.
* **Monthly Income:** Enter numeric value.
* **Company Name**
* **Company Type:** (e.g., private, government)
* **Employment Start and End Dates**
* **Office Address**

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image9.png)

## Step 10: Verify Income Details

### **Fields to Fill:**
* Monthly Income
* Income Source
* Employer Details

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image10.png)

## Step 11: Provide Loan Details

### **Fields to Fill:**
* **Loan Amount:** Enter the desired amount.
* **Loan Tenure:** Choose the duration (e.g., 2 Years, 3 Years).

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image11.png)

## Step 12: Additional Details Submission

### **Key Requirements:**
* **Aadhaar Number**
* **Property Identified?**
* **Property Type**
* **Employment Sector**
* **Property Value**
* **Net Income**
* **Deductions**
* **Expected Retirement Age**

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image12.png)

## Step 13: Document Upload for KYC

### **KYC Documents:**
* Aadhaar Card
* PAN Card

### **Conditional Documents:**
* Last Year Salary Slip (if applicable)
* Form 16
* Bank Statement (Last 6 Months)
* Address Proof

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image13.png)
![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image14.png)
## Step 14: Application Submission Confirmation

### **Key Information Displayed:**
* **Application ID:** Unique identifier for tracking.
* **Support Information:** Contact within 24 to 48 hours.
* **Customer Service:**
  * **Phone:** 1800 266 7576
  * **Email:** support@ruloans.com

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/app/loans/home-loan/dmi/image15.png)
