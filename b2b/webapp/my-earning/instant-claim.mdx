## **Steps to Claim a Lead Instantly**

### **Step 1: Login to the B2B WebApp**

- The **DSA logs in** using their credentials.
- The **B2B Dashboard** will appear.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image1.png)

### **Step 2: Navigate to Instant Claim Section**

- Click on the **hamburger menu** (â˜°).
- Select **My Earnings \> Instant Claim**.
- If no claim is initiated, the **Instant Payout Page** will display a message with a **"Click Here"** link.
- Click on **"Click Here"** to proceed to the **Applications Page**.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image2.png)

### **Step 3: Select and Claim Leads**

- On the **Applications Page**, all eligible leads are listed.
- Find the **Disbursed leads** that are ready for claim processing.
- Click the **Claim Button** next to the preferred lead.

![](https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image3.png)
