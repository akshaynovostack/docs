# **Direct Lead Claim Process**

## **Objective**

To allow DSAs to claim eligible leads using the **Instant Claim** feature for faster payout processing.

## **Steps to Claim a Lead Instantly**

### **Step 1: Login to the B2B WebApp**

* The **DSA logs in** using their credentials.  
* The **B2B Dashboard** will appear.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image1.png"/>

### **Step 2: Navigate to Instant Claim Section**

* Click on the **hamburger menu** (☰).  
* Select **My Earnings > Instant Claim**.  
* If no claim is initiated, the **Instant Payout Page** will display a message with a **"Click Here"** link.  
* Click on **"Click Here"** to proceed to the **Applications Page**.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image2.png"/>

### **Step 3: Select and Claim Leads**

* On the **Applications Page**, all eligible leads are listed.  
* Find the **Disbursed leads** that are ready for claim processing.  
* Click the **Claim Button** next to the preferred lead.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image3.png"/>

**Step 4: Claimed file on Instant Payout page**

* If the file is claimed from the application tracker it will be shown here as **“claimed”**.

<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image4.png"/>

**Step 5: All Instant claims will be shown in the Instant Claim Section in the CRM.** 

*  **CRM→** **Finance → Claims → Instant Claims**  
<img  src="https://ruloans-bucket.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/docs/b2b/webapp/my-earnings/instant-claim/image5.png"/>
* Once the claim button is clicked, the lead will be transferred to CRM under:  
  * **Finance → Claims → Payout → Processed → Sent to ERP .**

---

### **Conclusion**

By following these structured steps, organizations can efficiently enable **Instant Claims** for products, authorize DSAs, and facilitate direct lead claims, ensuring a smooth and **faster payout process**. 🚀
